Origin

Boba (aka bubble tea) began in Taiwan in the late 1980s, when creamy milk tea met springy tapioca pearls. The combo traveled the world and became a cozy, customizable classic.

At Boba, we honor those roots with high-mountain teas and time-tested methods. Our pearls are cooked in small batches for a caramel-soft chew, then married with fresh-brewed tea and your choice of milk and sweetness.
